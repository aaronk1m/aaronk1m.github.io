Why I chose to major in Management Information Systems

I chose to major in Management Information Systems because of its dynamic blend between the business world and technology. Through MIS, I hope to become knowledgable of cybersecurity, project management, and data analysis. The versatility of MIS was appealing to me through its possiblity of working in various industries. Overall, I felt that MIS was a major worth investing in, where I would find a promising career reshaping businesses in an increasingly digital world.
